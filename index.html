About Us

Capitol Medical Center, Inc. (CMCI) has been a beacon of medical excellence in the Philippines for more than five decades. Established on March 19, 1970, CMCI today is a leading private hospital with a 200-bed capacity and a comprehensive suite of healthcare and wellness solutions.

Through the years, it has brought together some of the most talented medical professionals from all over the world.

CMCI has the distinction of being home to the Philippines’ first successful kidney transplant in the 1970s, performed just five months after it opened its doors to the public. Since then, it has successfully helped raise the bar in healthcare in the country. It continues to make a difference in the life of the people through the delivery of accessible care and compassion.

Accredited as a Center for Excellence by the Philippine Health Insurance Corp (PHIC), CMCI strives to make a difference in the lives of patients and the community through consistently adhering to the “Right Care, Right Here” philosophy of service — its unique brand of service transcending from the 1970s to the present.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We consistently employ current knowledge and theory while understanding personal limits, integrate judgment and the customer’s perspective, embrace advancement and challenges, and work toward acquiring new knowledge. We adhere to excellence through great work performance, first-rate competence and professionalism, advanced medical knowledge and technology, active integrative and collaborative practices, determination and resolve to excel, continuous improvement, and maintaining a high level of patient care.

Teamwork

We get things done through the collective and coordinated efforts of the CMC staff with diverse talents, backgrounds, ideas, and experiences that offer solutions and benefits to customers. We observe teamwork through searching and nurturing untapped talent, valuing their contributions, building new leaders, open communication, recognition and encouragement, and a “Together we stand” mindset.

Integrity

We strictly adhere to high ethical and professional principles, truthfulness, and fairness. We manifest integrity through abiding by rules, regulations, and applicable laws, as well as our ideals, company mission, and core values. We also recognize our limitations and the limits of expertise, making referrals appropriately. We practice values and professional ethical standards, truth and honesty in action, and high standards of behavior in our conduct of hospital business. We also value and embrace diversity.
